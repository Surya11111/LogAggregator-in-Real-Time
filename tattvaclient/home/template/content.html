How it Works

Data processing

By observing the credit card access pattern of a specific user and by comparing each access with his historic access pattern, flag the access as fraudulent or normal, before the transaction is complete or settled. by continuously calculating the average price of a specific stock's price against the competitors stock price.

Execution

On a stock price data feed, by continuously calculating the average price of a specific stock's price against the competitors stock price, we can trigger event if the price is sharply raising/dropping due unexpected event at stock's firm, which can help the trader to decide if he/she has to got long or short on the stock

Future Scope

Application of complex event processing is growing in healthcare to provide solutions for personalized medicine. Computers and information systems can be used for collecting patient data in healthcare. Monitoring patterns in these inputs can help in providing precautionary medication to patients.
